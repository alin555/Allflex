Congratulations! A new team member is about to join your team.

This is the first of the Onboarding Tool Kit for Manager. its objective is to provide guidelines for a successful professional onboarding of new team members joining Allflex.
This will help you ensure they get all the information they need to understand the business and fulfill their role efficiently.

Direct Manager

Buddy

The direct manager is responsible for a successful onboarding experience:
  • Assigning a Buddy (other team member or yourself)
  • Planning and delivering the onboarding journey – role-based training and meetings
  • Setting training goals and professional Key Performance Indicators (KPI’s)
  • Aligning training expectations
Assigning a buddy is not mandatory, but highly recommended.
The buddy has the direct responsibility for the professional onboarding.
Hold a meeting with the Buddy to ask if they are willing to fill this role:
  • Mentoring, being the content expert
  • Providing guidance throughout the onboarding process, from the first day to the last
  • Evaluating the new team member’s performance in the onboarding process
