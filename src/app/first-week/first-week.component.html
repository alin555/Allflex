Allflex Global Onboarding Resources Site

The Global Onboarding Resources Site provides new Allflex team members the knowledge needed to successfully integrate with the business, as well as expose and share existing Animal Health colleagues to Allflex portfolio, products, and solutions. The site is categorized into two main sections:

1. Onboarding Learning Pathways 2. The Allflex Academy

Onboarding Learning Pathways

The Allflex Academy

The Learning Pathways includes a series of self-guided learning courses divided into three main business areas representing the Allflex Livestock Intelligence solutions: Monitoring, Milking Automation, and Identification. Each learning pathway includes a focus area module with the capability for learners to self-track their course completion.

Since many of the courses on this site are tracked by the Learning Management System, you can receive completion reports of the team members by reaching out to the Allflex Global Customer Experience Team.

The Allflex Academy has global training resources for Allflex Portfolio, including a variety of tutorials, videos, and presentations.
This resource is recommended for colleagues who are already familiar with Allflex and are interested in learning more about our products and solutions.
