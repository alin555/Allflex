Welcome Meeting- Key Points

  • Introduce yourself – professionally and personally
  • Get to know your new employees – ask them to share their expectations, concerns, and wishes. Listen to them – this is a great opportunity to build a trusting relationship
  • Share with them their first week’s agenda, see that it’s clear and they feel comfortable with it. Print it for them so they would have it handy
  • Introduce them to Allflex’s onboarding learning management system and how it can support their onboarding process
  • Ensure they have received everything they need and that everything is in order
