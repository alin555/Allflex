What is a Professional Onboarding Agenda?

The professional onboarding agenda is an organized list of activities that sets clear expectations for of the learning journey the new team member needs to accomplish and when, during the onboarding process.

How to Create a Professional Onboarding Agenda?

1

Create a list of topics for the training agenda based on the role the new team member will fulfill

2

Adjust the agenda according to the knowledge and experience of the new team member. A new team member with a farming background or relevant knowledge from the industry will most likely need a shorter onboarding process

3

Add global learning materials to the agenda by using units from the Allflex Global Onboarding Resources Site. This will provide the new team member with the foundational necessary knowledge they need

4

Include local learning materials to the agenda. This include relevant learning units of your region such as competition in the local market, common type of farms, and relevant internal regional processes

5

Frame the timeline for the training. Define when the new team member should be able to successfully apply all skills and knowledge required in the training

6

Set the training KPI’s per each month. For example: a ‘Completed’ status in all the theoretical learning units on the first month, and successfully accomplished practical tasks on the second month.
