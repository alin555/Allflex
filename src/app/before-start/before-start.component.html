Before Start

Congratulations!

2-3 weeks before their official start date is a good time to start preparing, along with your HR Person, to ensure everything is in place for the new team member.

The new employee will receive a message with an attached PDF file to welcome them and briefly explain about their first day at work.

1

Discuss options with the HR person and Choose a Buddy. Hold a meeting with the Buddy to ask if they are willing to fill this role and to discuss what it means.

2

Adjust the onboarding first week agenda and the full onboarding plan using the plan template:
  • Consult with your HR person
  • Try to schedule meetings in advance

3

After they sign the contract, schedule time in your calendar for the following meetings with your new employees:
  • Welcome meeting on the first day
  • Role Introduction meeting
  • Weekly meeting

4

Contact your new employee: call or send a nice short text message to congratulate them on their decision and express your expectation.
